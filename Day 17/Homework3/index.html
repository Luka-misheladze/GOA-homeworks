GEORGIA

You should visit Georgia for its incredibly diverse landscapes,
ranging from the high Caucasus Mountains to Black Sea beaches, its millennia-old wine culture,
delicious and unique cuisine, and rich history and architecture.
Georgia also offers vibrant city life, especially Tbilisi, and a reputation for warm hospitality,
making it an affordable, developing, and welcoming destination for various types of travelers.

What makes Georgia unique?

Nestled between Europe and Asia, Georgia is a land of breathtaking mountains, ancient history, and warm hospitality. From the snow-capped peaks of the Caucasus to the Black Sea coastline, every corner of Georgia has its own magic.

Traditional Georgian Food

Georgia is world-famous for its delicious food and wine. From the cheesy khachapuri to the juicy dumplings called khinkali, every meal tells a story of tradition. Don’t miss trying local wine — Georgia is the birthplace of winemaking.

Adventure in the Caucasus

For nature lovers, Georgia is paradise. You can hike in Kazbegi, ski in Gudauri, or explore hidden waterfalls in Racha. The Caucasus mountains are a dream for adventurers.
