Old cars

1 - Mercedes Benz




- The Mercedes-Benz W124 was a line of executive cars produced by Daimler-Benz from 1984 to 1997.
- It's known for its robust build quality, reliability, and advanced engineering,
- particularly its multi-link rear suspension and high-strength steel usage.

2 - Mercedes AMG




- The W212 and S212 Mercedes-Benz E-Class series is the fourth generation
- of the E-Class range of executive cars which was produced by Mercedes-Benz
- between 2009 and 2016 as the successor to the W211 E-Class.
- The body styles of the range are either four-door sedan/saloon (W212) or a five-door estate/wagon (S212).
